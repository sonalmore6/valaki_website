Repair Washer

Repair washers are specially designed washers with a larger outer diameter used to repair damaged, oversized, or worn-out holes in materials. They help restore proper load distribution and provide a secure fastening surface.

These washers are widely used in maintenance, automotive repairs, sheet metal work, and construction applications where standard washers are not sufficient.
