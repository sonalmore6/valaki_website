SS Allen CSK Bolt

SS Allen CSK (Countersunk) Bolts are precision fasteners made from high-quality stainless steel such as SS 304 and SS 316. Designed with a flat countersunk head and internal hex drive, these bolts provide a flush finish after installation, making them ideal for applications where a smooth surface is required. These bolts offer excellent corrosion resistance, durability, and strength, making them suitable for use in marine environments, food processing industries, chemical plants, and outdoor applications.

Material: SS 304 / SS 316 M3 – M20 Corrosion Resistant DIN 7991 Polished / Natural Finish
