SS304 Allen CSK Bolt

SS304 Allen CSK (Countersunk) Bolts are high-quality stainless steel fasteners designed for applications where corrosion resistance and a smooth surface finish are essential. These bolts feature a flat countersunk head with an internal hex (Allen) drive, allowing them to sit flush with the surface after installation. Manufactured from SS304 (A2 grade) stainless steel, these bolts provide excellent resistance to rust, moisture, and chemicals. They are widely used in food processing, marine, construction, and outdoor applications where durability and hygiene are important.

Material: SS304 Grade: A2-70 M3 – M20 DIN 7991 Natural / Polished Finish
