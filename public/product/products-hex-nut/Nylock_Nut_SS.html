Nylock Nut – Stainless Steel

Stainless steel nylon insert lock nuts combine the vibration-resistant locking performance of a Nylock nut with the excellent corrosion resistance of stainless steel. The nylon collar at the top of the nut grips the bolt threads through elastic deformation, providing a reliable self-locking action that maintains joint integrity in dynamic and vibrating environments without any additional locking hardware.

Available in SS 304 (A2) and SS 316 (A4), these nuts are ideal for marine structures, food processing equipment, outdoor electrical panels, and chemical plant installations where both locking and corrosion resistance are required. The passivated stainless steel body ensures a clean, hygienic finish suitable for food-grade applications.
