Hex Nut – High Tensile

High tensile hex nuts are engineered for heavy-duty applications where standard grade nuts cannot withstand the required load levels. Manufactured from alloy steel and heat-treated to achieve elevated mechanical properties, these nuts are specifically designed to be matched with Grade 8.8 and 10.9 bolts in demanding structural steel connections, heavy machinery, and automotive assemblies.

The superior strength of high tensile nuts ensures joint integrity under high-load and dynamic loading conditions including vibration, shock loads, and thermal cycling. Their hardened surface resists thread stripping and galling, providing consistent clamping performance over the full service life of the assembly.
