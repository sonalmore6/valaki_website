Weld Nuts

Projection Weld Nut

Projection weld nuts are resistance-welded fasteners with small projections on the nut face that focus welding current and create strong attachment points on sheet metal. They provide high strength and precise alignment for threaded connections.

Commonly used in automotive panels, industrial equipment, and appliance assemblies where durable and vibration-resistant fastening is required.
