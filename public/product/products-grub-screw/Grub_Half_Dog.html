Grub Screw

Socket Point Grub Screw

Socket set screw designed with an internal hex (Allen) drive for secure and precise tightening. Commonly used in machinery, automotive, and industrial assemblies where a compact, headless fastening solution is required.

Provides reliable holding strength and flush surface installation, making it ideal for applications involving shafts, collars, pulleys, and gears where space is limited and a clean finish is preferred.

Grade: All M3 – M12 MS Zinc / SS304 DIN 913 / DIN 914 / DIN 915 / DIN 916 Hex Socket Drive
