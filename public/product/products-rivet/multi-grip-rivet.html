Multi-Grip Rivet

Multi-Grip Rivets (also known as wide grip rivets) are advanced blind fasteners designed to accommodate a wide range of material thicknesses with a single rivet size. This reduces the need for multiple rivet lengths, simplifying inventory and installation.

These rivets provide strong clamping force, excellent hole filling, and vibration resistance. They are ideal for applications where material thickness varies or where a reliable and flexible fastening solution is required. Multi-grip rivets are widely used in automotive, electrical enclosures, sheet metal fabrication, and construction industries.
