Wood Screw

Wood screws feature a coarse, widely-spaced thread that grips timber fibres tightly for maximum pull-out strength. The tapered shank and sharp tip allow clean entry into solid wood, plywood, and MDF without splitting.

Countersunk head provides a flush or recessed finish ideal for furniture and joinery work. Available in zinc-plated mild steel, stainless steel, and bright zinc-plated (BZP) finish for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Coarse Thread M3 – M10 12 – 100 mm Zinc / SS / BZP
